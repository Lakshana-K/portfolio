About me,
honestly.

I’m a Year 3 Information Technology student at Temasek Polytechnic who spends most of her time thinking about how things work and why they’re designed the way they are. Outside of school, I like reading books, philosophising about things, journalling, and collecting little trinkets that probably mean more to me than they should.

This site is mostly just a place for me to keep things I want to remember. Projects I’ve worked on, books I’ve loved, thoughts I had at inconvenient times, and little pieces of my life I don’t want to lose.

Lately, I’ve been really interested in technology, philosophy, journalling, tea tasting, hiking, running, and learning how to slow down and live a little more intentionally. The list keeps growing faster than I can keep up with it.

I still don’t fully know what I’m doing yet, but I think that’s kind of the point.
